EMSR097: Floods in Bangladesh

Event Time (UTC): 2014-08-10 00:00 Event Time (LOC): 2014-08-09 18:00 Event Type: Flood Activation Time (UTC): 2014-08-22 12:43 Activation Status: Closed
Affected Countries/Territories:
People's Republic of Bangladesh
Service Output: 10 maps Reference: 5 maps Delineation: 5 maps Area Descriptor: Northern part of Bangladesh and upstream Indian States of Assam, Meghalaya, West Bengal and Sikkim
Authorised User:
EC Services|DG ECHO
Activation Reason:
Starting from the 10th of August, 2014 the northern part of Bangladesh and upstream Indian States of Assam, Meghalaya, West Bengal and Sikkim have been experiencing heavy rainfall for few days. As a result, districts in the Northwest along the Brahmaputra river have been experiencing low level flooding, causing a rising trend of the major river system and, consequently, some low-lying areas have been inundated.
Requested Product: Flooding area extent (Reference and delineation)
Consult the Online Manual for guidance on the use and interpretation of the products delivered.
